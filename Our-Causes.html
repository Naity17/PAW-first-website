Our Causes

The life of street dogs in Bulgaria is hearbreaking. Therfore, we decided to take matter in our own hands and fight the good fight for already eight years. Here are our causes.

Building "The Farm"

Our primary goal for a long time was to have our own space, which we can turn into a shelter for the animals we saved from the streets. It was a difficult task but with soon enough, with the help of our families and supporters, we managed to find the perfect place. It requires a lot of work to be completed but we currently have enough outdoor space for the dogs to run, enough cages for the ones, which need rest to heal and plenty of areas where the doggies can relax.

Taking care of sick and mistreated animals

Every day in Bulgaria there are cases of animal abuse or pet abandonment. Both of which can lead to extremely difficult to treat diseases or malformations. Therefore, we dedicate our time and efforts to those who need us most.
